# Stocking Stuffers for Hikers

Small, trail-ready gifts that add joy to every mile.

## Trail treats and rituals
- Honey wafers, energy chews, and instant espresso packets
- Pocket notebook and pencil for summit registers and route notes
- Tiny gratitude token or patch for a pack strap

## Maintenance and repair
- Mini roll of Leukotape for hotspots
- Reusable wire twist ties for hanging lights or bundling cord
- Pocket-sized gear wipes for quick cleanups

## Cozy comforts
- Lightweight camp socks reserved for sleeping
- Soft beanie that fits under a helmet or hood
- Lip balm with SPF and a carabiner loop
