# Backpacking Gifts Under $25

Thoughtful backpacking gear does not have to break the bank. These picks keep packs light and budgets happy.

## Lightweight kitchen wins
- Collapsible silicone bowl for trail meals without bulky pots
- Long-handled titanium spork that reaches into freezer bags
- Mini spice kit with refillable vials for campsite flavor

## Safety and navigation add-ons
- Whistle and signal mirror combo that tucks into a hip belt pocket
- Compact roll of flagging tape for quick campsite marking
- Button compass paired with a laminated route card

## Comfort upgrades
- Cushioned merino socks that dry fast and resist blisters
- Mini closed-cell sit pad for damp logs or rocky summits
- Soft microfiber camp towel sized for face and hands
