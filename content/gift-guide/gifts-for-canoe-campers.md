# Gifts for Canoe Campers

Paddle-friendly presents that balance portage efficiency with on-water comfort.

## Camp kitchen for the boat
- Waterproof barrel liner bags to separate pantry staples
- Folding grill grate that fits over fire rings or river cobbles
- Wide pot lifter and heat-resistant gloves for fire cooking

## Portage-ready comfort
- Packable camp chair that straps to a thwart during crossings
- Quick-dry camp shoes with aggressive tread for slick landings
- Ultralight tarp with reflective guylines for fast riverside shelters

## Safety on lake and river routes
- Throw rope with padded bag and compact carabiner
- Laminated route maps stored in a low-profile deck case
- Bright bow and stern lights for early launches and foggy mornings
