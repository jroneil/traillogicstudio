# Cold-Weather Gear Guide

Layer smart, stay dry, and keep moving when the temperature drops with these cold-weather picks.

## Base and mid layers
- Merino grid hoodie that vents heat but insulates when damp
- Synthetic quarter-zip with thumb loops for easy layering
- Wool liner gloves for dexterity under shells

## Weather protection
- Waterproof breathable mittens with removable fleece liners
- Windproof bivy sack to boost sleeping bag warmth on frigid nights
- Neck gaiter that doubles as a beanie or face cover

## Trail nutrition and hydration
- Insulated bottle sleeve to keep water from freezing
- Wide-mouth thermos for soup and hot drinks at camp
- High-calorie snack pack with nut butter, chews, and drink mix
