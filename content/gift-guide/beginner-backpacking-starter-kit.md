# Beginner Backpacking Starter Kit

A simple, confidence-building kit for hikers taking their first overnight leap.

## Sleep system basics
- 40-50L pack with supportive hip belt and intuitive pockets
- Three-season sleeping bag with hood and draft collar
- Lightweight closed-cell foam pad that doubles as a sit pad

## Cook and water setup
- Canister stove with push-button ignition
- 750-900ml pot with graduated marks and a secure lid
- Squeeze-style water filter plus a dirty water reservoir

## Trail readiness
- Compact first aid kit with blister care, meds, and gauze
- Headlamp with spare batteries and lockout switch
- Map, compass, and beginner-friendly navigation field guide
