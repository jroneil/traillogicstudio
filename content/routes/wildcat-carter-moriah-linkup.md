# Wildcat-Carters-Moriah Linkup

## Overview
Full traverse from Pinkham Notch over the Wildcats, Carters, and Moriah for a committing, peak-packed outing parallel to the Presidential Range.

## Distance + Gain (Approx)
20 miles, ~7,000 feet gain.

## Difficulty
Very strenuous, best for strong hikers comfortable with steep ascents and rugged tread.

## Itinerary Suggestions
- Start pre-dawn from Pinkham via Wildcat Ridge Trail to beat the crowds on the AT section near the hut.
- Overnight at Imp Shelter or stealth-free itinerary with a vehicle shuttle to Gorham.
- Keep breaks efficient to manage daylight on a one-day push.

## Warnings
- Steep ledges near Wildcat A/B demand caution when wet.
- Exposure to wind and lightning along open viewpoints.

## Bailouts
- Descent options via Nineteen Mile Brook or Stony Brook if pace lags.
- Use the gondola at Wildcat only during operating hours; do not rely on it.

## Weather Considerations
- Expect rapid shifts mirroring the Presidentials; check MWOBS forecast before committing.
- Heat indexes can climb in summer; hydrate aggressively.
