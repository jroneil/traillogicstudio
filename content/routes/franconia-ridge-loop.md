# Franconia Ridge Loop

## Overview
Classic White Mountains loop over Little Haystack, Lincoln, and Lafayette with big alpine views and easy logistics from Franconia Notch.

## Distance + Gain (Approx)
8.9 miles, ~3,900 feet gain.

## Difficulty
Challenging day hike with sustained ascent and exposed ridgeline travel.

## Itinerary Suggestions
- Start early from Lafayette Place via Falling Waters Trail for morning waterfall views.
- Tag the ridge clockwise to keep the steepest slabs on the ascent.
- Pause at Greenleaf Hut for water and a snack before descending Old Bridle Path.

## Tips
- Trekking poles help on the rocky descent.
- Parking fills quickly; arrive before 7 a.m. on busy weekends.
- Bring a wind layer for the exposed ridge.

## Warnings
- Above-treeline weather changes fast; thunderstorms roll in with little warning.
- Slippery rock on Falling Waters after rain.

## Bailouts
- Turn around at Little Haystack if clouds build.
- From Lafayette, descend Greenleaf Trail to the hut and drop to I-93 via Old Bridle Path.

## Weather Considerations
- Expect strong winds on the ridge even on calm days.
- Winter/shoulder seasons often require traction for ice.
