# Long Trail: Mount Mansfield Ridge Walk

## Overview
Alpine traverse across Vermont's highest peak from Underhill State Park to Smugglers' Notch with sweeping Green Mountain views.

## Distance + Gain (Approx)
7 miles, ~2,800 feet gain.

## Difficulty
Challenging day hike with exposed scrambles and sensitive alpine zones.

## Itinerary Suggestions
- Ascend the Sunset Ridge Trail to the Chin, then follow the Long Trail south across the ridgeline features.
- Descend via the Hellbrook or Profanity Trail to Barnes Camp with a vehicle shuttle.
- Start early to secure parking and avoid midday heat on the ridge.

## Tips
- Stay on rock to protect fragile alpine plants.
- Trekking poles are helpful for the steep descent.
- Carry ample water; few sources once above treeline.

## Warnings
- Exposure and scrambles on the Adam's Apple and Forehead sections.
- Crowds on fair weekends—be patient on narrow scrambles.

## Bailouts
- Profanity Trail offers a quicker descent to the Auto Toll Road area.
- You can reverse to Sunset Ridge if weather deteriorates before committing to the ridge.

## Weather Considerations
- Rapid weather shifts; fog and wind common.
- Thunderstorms pose lightning risk on exposed ridge—descend immediately if clouds build.
