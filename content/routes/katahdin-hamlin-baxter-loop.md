# Katahdin Hamlin & Baxter Peaks Loop

## Overview
Loop from Roaring Brook over Katahdin's Hamlin Peak and Baxter Peak via the Saddle for big alpine views with less exposure than the Knife Edge.

## Distance + Gain (Approx)
10.5 miles, ~4,100 feet gain.

## Difficulty
Strenuous alpine hike with boulder hopping and steep grades.

## Itinerary Suggestions
- Ascend Hamlin Ridge early for morning light and fewer crowds.
- Traverse the tableland to Baxter Peak, then descend via the Saddle and Chimney Pond.
- Secure parking reservation for Roaring Brook in advance.

## Tips
- Carry plenty of water; limited sources above treeline.
- Trekking poles help on the Saddle scree.
- Stop at Chimney Pond Ranger Station to check weather and trail updates.

## Warnings
- Alpine environment with no shade—sun protection essential.
- Loose rock on Hamlin Ridge and the Saddle can roll underfoot.

## Bailouts
- Descend via Saddle Trail after Baxter Peak if weather deteriorates.
- Chimney Pond Campground offers shelter reservations if you need to wait out conditions.

## Weather Considerations
- Summit can be extremely windy; avoid in thunderstorms.
- Early season snow/ice common—check for traction requirements with rangers.
