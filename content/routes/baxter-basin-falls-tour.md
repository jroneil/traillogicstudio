# Baxter Basin Falls Tour

## Overview
Relaxed hike from Roaring Brook exploring Katahdin's basins with stops at Chimney Pond, Basin Ponds, and scenic waterfalls.

## Distance + Gain (Approx)
9 miles, ~1,200 feet gain.

## Difficulty
Easy-moderate; well-graded trails with gradual elevation.

## Itinerary Suggestions
- Hike to Chimney Pond for views of the Great Basin cliffs.
- Continue to Basin Ponds and loop past Sandy Stream Pond for wildlife spotting.
- Reserve a parking spot at Roaring Brook and take your time with photography.

## Tips
- Moose sightings are common near Sandy Stream Pond—bring binoculars.
- Carry a map showing side trails to avoid missing junctions.
- Pack a lunch for the Chimney Pond picnic area.

## Warnings
- Boardwalks can be slippery; tread carefully.
- Limited cell service—carry paper maps.

## Bailouts
- Shorten the outing by turning around at Chimney Pond if weather builds.

## Weather Considerations
- Basin often cooler than trailhead; bring a light layer.
- Rain can swell small streams; waterproof footwear recommended.
