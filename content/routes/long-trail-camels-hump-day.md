# Long Trail: Camel's Hump Day Loop

## Overview
Scenic loop to Camel's Hump from the Burrows/Forest City side combining a Long Trail ascent with a quieter descent.

## Distance + Gain (Approx)
6 miles, ~2,300 feet gain.

## Difficulty
Moderate; rocky summit cone with some scrambling.

## Itinerary Suggestions
- Ascend via Burrows Trail to the Long Trail junction and summit for panoramic views.
- Descend via the Forest City Trail to create a loop.
- Start early for parking and to enjoy sunrise or morning light.

## Warnings
- Summit ledges are exposed; avoid in lightning.
- Trails can be icy well into spring.

## Bailouts
- Return via Burrows if weather shifts on top.

## Weather Considerations
- Summit often sits in the clouds; check forecasts for visibility.
- Winter requires traction and often microspikes or crampons near the top.
