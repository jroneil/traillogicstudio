# Carter-Moriah Traverse

## Overview
North-to-south traverse across the Carter-Moriah Range with sweeping views into the Presidential Range and quiet forest stretches.

## Distance + Gain (Approx)
14.5 miles, ~4,900 feet gain.

## Difficulty
Hard day hike or relaxed overnight with steep climbs and frequent ups and downs along the ridge.

## Itinerary Suggestions
- Spot a car at Pinkham Notch and start in Gorham via the Carter-Moriah Trail.
- Tag Moriah, the Carters, and descend Nineteen Mile Brook to finish.
- Overnight option at Imp Shelter or Carter Notch Hut for a two-day tour.

## Warnings
- Ridge can be slick in rain or frost; traction may be needed in shoulder seasons.
- Limited escape options once committed to the crest.

## Bailouts
- Descend Stony Brook Trail after Moriah for a shorter day.
- Exit via Carter Dome Trail to 19-Mile Brook if weather deteriorates.

## Weather Considerations
- Fog often lingers on the east-facing slopes; keep navigation tools handy.
- Summer thunderstorms can build quickly over the Presidentials.
