# Pemi Loop Lite

## Overview
Shortened Pemigewasset loop linking Franconia Ridge, Garfield, and the Twins for a big-mountain overnight without the full 30-mile commitment.

## Distance + Gain (Approx)
22 miles, ~6,500 feet gain.

## Difficulty
Strenuous overnight backpack with extended ridgeline travel and rocky tread.

## Itinerary Suggestions
- Day 1: Lafayette Place to Garfield Ridge Campsite via Franconia Ridge for a short but steep first day.
- Day 2: Continue over Garfield and the Twins, then descend Twinway to Lincoln Woods.
- Optional side trip to Galehead Hut for water and snacks.

## Tips
- Carry a small bear-resistant storage solution for the campsite platforms.
- Refill at AMC huts when in season to reduce water weight.
- Plan for limited cell coverage.

## Warnings
- Garfield Ridge includes slick ledges in wet weather.
- Campsite platforms may fill on peak weekends—arrive early.

## Bailouts
- From Garfield Ridge, descend Gale River Trail to the road.
- Twinway to Zealand Trail offers a shorter exit to the Zealand trailhead.

## Weather Considerations
- Clouds can obscure navigation on the open sections; bring map and compass.
- Heat and humidity can be taxing—schedule breaks accordingly.
